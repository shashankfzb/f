Privacy Policy & Security Terms

This document explains how honestkharcha handles data, security, and user privacy in compliance with Google Play policies and applicable laws.

Last Updated: 1 January 2026

honestkharcha (“Application”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is a mobile application that allows users to manually record and categorize personal expenses for self-management purposes.

1. Application Purpose

honestkharcha is intended solely for personal expense tracking and categorization. The Application does not provide financial, investment, tax, accounting, or legal advice.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

The Application does not require account creation and does not collect personal identifiers such as name, email address, phone number, or address.

2.2 Expense & Category Data

Users may manually enter expense amounts, categories, notes, dates, and budget data. All such data:

  • Is stored locally on the user’s device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is not accessible to us in any form

2.3 Automatically Collected Technical Data

Limited non-identifiable technical data (such as app version, device type, and crash diagnostics) may be collected strictly to improve stability and reliability.

3. Data Storage & Retention

User-entered data remains on the device unless deleted by the user. honestkharcha does not maintain cloud backups or external databases of user data.

4. Security Practices

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards appropriate for a local-storage application, including OS-level sandboxing.

However, no electronic storage method is completely secure. Users are responsible for maintaining device security (PINs, biometrics, OS updates).

5. Data Breach & Incident Handling

As no centralized storage exists, the risk of large-scale data breaches is minimal. If a security vulnerability is identified, remediation will be provided through application updates and notifications where required.

6. Third-Party Services

honestkharcha may use trusted third-party services such as Google Play Services or Firebase Crash Reporting / Analytics (if enabled). These services operate under their own privacy policies.

7. User Responsibilities

  • Ensuring accuracy of entered expense data
  • Maintaining backups if needed
  • Securing the device against unauthorized access

8. Children’s Privacy

honestkharcha is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

9. Limitation of Liability

The Application is provided “as is” and “as available”. We are not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages, including loss of data or financial records.
